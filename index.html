Putting Away Our Swords

After our first convention in 2019, we were gearing up for Swords and Sorcery, the theme for LIFC 2020. Unbeknownst to us, we’d soon be fighting an entirely different battle. The COVID-19 pandemic is tough, but we’re tougher. Despite all odds, we pushed through. We social-distance. We found a vaccine. We created a new normal.

Yet as we adapted to our new lives, not everything is back to normal. We’ve lost many opportunities to reconnect with our furry friends and loved ones. Meetups were cancelled, conventions were postponed. Furry gatherings as we know it are gone, for now.

It’s time to bring everyone back, safely.

LIFC Online

LIFC is taking things online. While physical gatherings are still not feasible and safe, we can still do a lot! There’s going to be a Dealer’s Den, panels, VRChat, and more! All this is coming to you this November 13th as LIFC Online. And most importantly, we’re going to do it in the comfort and safety of home.

To keep this event accessible to all, attendee tickets to the event are completely free. All you need is a Discord account, and you’ll get to reconnect with people you haven’t seen in a while.

The past year and a half was hard, and you’ve done well. It’s time to sit back, relax, and reconnect. Welcome home.

With all the love and support in the world,
The LIFC Team
